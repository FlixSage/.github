# FlixSage

  FlixSage is a movie and TV discovery brand focused on helping people decide what to watch, faster.
  We turn screenshots, posters, and short prompts into clear viewing insights so discovery feels simple, not overwhelming.

  ## Our Mission

  Make entertainment discovery more useful, more intuitive, and less time-consuming for everyday viewers.

  ## What FlixSage Delivers

  - Fast title identification from visual or text input
  - Helpful movie and show context in one place
  - Better watch decisions through concise, relevant details
  - Personal discovery flows designed for real viewing habits

  ## What We Value

  - Clarity over noise
  - Speed with quality
  - Practical product decisions
  - Continuous improvement driven by real user behavior

  ## Who We Serve

  - Viewers who want quick, confident watch choices
  - Fans trying to identify unfamiliar scenes or titles
  - People actively curating their next watchlist
